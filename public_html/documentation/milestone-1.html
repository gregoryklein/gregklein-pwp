Greg Klein's PWP - Milestone 1

Purpose

A portfolio designed to show my web development skills and link to my work.

Audience

Potential employers, web development colleagues, my followers on social media

Goal

Acquire full-time employment as a full stack web developer at a local startup.



Persona 1

Sam White

35 years old

Hiring manager for Zuum, a local bike share startup looking to expand nationally. They recently received $10 million in VC funding and are hiring a full-time, in-house full stack web developer. This position will require remaking and maintaining their currently buggy site to provide a responsive, more intuitive UX for customers.

Technology

  • Cell Phone: Samsung Galaxy S9
  • Work Laptop: Acer Laptop Aspire 5 (Windows 10 64-Bit, Google Chrome, Intel Core i5, 8GB DDR4 RAM, 256GB SSD, Intel HD Graphics 620, 15.6" screen)

Scenario

          Views my portfolio site and relevant work to decide whether I'm qualified to redesign and maintain the front and back end of their website.

Frustrations and Needs

  • Ignores applicants with poorly formatted or buggy code
  • Prefers applicants who specialize in responsive, mobile-first UI/UX
  • Reluctant to interview candidates who cannot handle both front end and back end web development
  • Feels pressured to hire someone who exceeds the job requirements due to shoddy work from contractor/freelance developers hired in the past

Behaviors and Attitudes

  • Rarely spends more than 5 minutes on each application because of the high volume of both local and out-of-state applicants
  • More likely to hire someone if he's met the developer at a community event, follows them on social media, or was recommended to hire them by a colleague

Goals

  • Sam usually inspects the site's source code for errors and proper formatting.
  • Sam checks to see if sites display properly on his Android phone.
  • Sam ranks the quality of work in relation to other job applicants' websites in a Google Docs table

User Story

          As a user, Sam must decide which applicant seems most qualified to remake Zuum's website using HTML, CSS, JS, PHP, and MySQL.
